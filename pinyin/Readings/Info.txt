This folder is used to store Incremental Reading Text (Readings).

Files can be:
  .txt
  .u8
  .htm
  .html
  .njx
  .wen

Files can be placed in subdirectorys and will appear as part of a tree heirachy.

A few sample files are included for testing and development and reference purposes.
The files are sourced from the University of Iowa's Chinese program.
they are copyrighted and are included here under fair usage rules.
See: http://www.uiowa.edu/~chnsrdng/index.html for details and more files.

Another great source of reading materials is:
http://blog.sina.com.cn/
